FAQ's

#1 What is SMARTATHON?

The objective is to identify talent and provide winners with guidelines and resources so their projects and ideas can actually evolve to produce real impact in the city as tangible applications, with the aim of creating the first model for Smart City mobile apps, web applications, software or hardware repository for the Shivamogga City. Participants should build innovative solutions that help in the transformation of Shivamogga city into a smart city.

#2 When and where is the event?

April 16-17,2016 at JNNCE, Shimoga, Karnataka 577204.

#3 How do I register?

Sign up via Venturesity by April 12th, go through the qualification process, and then receive an invite.

#4 I don't want to do any of this. Can I walk in?

Unfortunately, there are no on-the-spot registrations.

#5 How big can a team be?

This hackathon is strictly a team competition. Max. team size : 4 members.

#6 I don't have a team, what do I do?

Go through the qualification process as an individual and on the day of the hackathon, we will run a small matchmaking program during check-in to help individuals find/form teams.

#7 Is it free?

Absolutely! Just bring your unique ideas. Food is on us!

#8 I missed the deadline. Can I still apply?

Unfortunately, NO. But do keep a look out for similar events in the future.

#9 What are the steps I should undertake after being selected?

First of all, congratulations! Once you are selected, we will send you a detailed email comprising all the details.

#10 Should I get my own equipment to the Hackathon?

Yes! Do get your own laptops and charger, cellphone and charger, data cards, water-bottle, extension cords, adapters and others. Student ID is MUST. Wi-Fi will be provided to you by us.

#11 What if I don't know how to code?

Whether you are a complete amateur eager to learn, or a designer who wants to get your head round development a little more, you can attend the event!

#12 Who can attend?

All the coolest people interested in tech will be there. Open to all passionate Coders!

#13 What can I build?

Pretty much anything you want. Show your coding skills according to the given theme.

#14 Who owns the intellectual property on the work that was built at the event?

We will not assume any rights, intellectual or otherwise, over work that is developed as part of this hackathon. All creation rights will vest with the team that creates it.
Also, SMARTATHON requires that all teams honour all existing licenses on prior art and not violate any confidences, contracts, licenses, patents by bringing prior art to the event. You are responsible for respecting any property rights of third parties.

#15 What about Travel & Accomodation facilities?

Unfortunately, SMART-A-THON organisers will not provide any of the above. But go through our website for all the details related to Accomodation & Travel.
